Healthy Diet Tips: 5 Things To Keep In Mind For Healthy Weight Loss

Diet tips: Here's how to maintain a healthy diet for achieving weight loss.

If you are on a weight loss spree, then it is important for you to maintain a balanced diet and healthy exercise routine. You must set realistic goals to shed kilos that you can achieve in the weeks and months to come.

Do not pressurise or stress yourself out in order to achieve those goals, because when it comes to weight loss, it should be slow and in a healthy way, without disrupting your body and health. According to Health Practitioner, Nutritionist and certified Macrobiotic Health Coach, Shilpa Arora ND, one must aim to lose weight in a healthy way.
